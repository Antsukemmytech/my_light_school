About My Light School

As a seasoned educator and software developer, I, Emmanuel, drew upon my firsthand experience as an examination officer to create My Light School. This innovative platform addresses the long-standing challenges faced by schools in compiling and managing student results.

My Light School is the culmination of my passion for education and technology. Developed as part of my ALX Portfolio Project, this platform demonstrates my commitment to harnessing innovation to solve real-world problems in the edutech space.
